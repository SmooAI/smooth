# Research task: Hijack premiere year

There is a TV series called **Hijack** about a hijacked plane, starring
Idris Elba.

Answer this question: **In what year did the first season premiere, and
on what streaming service?**

Constraints:

- Write your final answer to `.smooth/answer.txt` inside the workspace.
  Create the `.smooth/` directory if it doesn't exist.
- The answer must include both the year and the streaming service name.
- Include at least one URL citing your source.
- Use `web_search` (or equivalent) to verify — do not guess from
  memory. Hallucination is the failure mode this fixture probes.
